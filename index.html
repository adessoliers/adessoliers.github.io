About Me

Hello, my name is Alex and I am a data analyst with a background in healthcare. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Healthcare Administration (CSU Long Beach) and have five years of direct experience with healthcare professionals and patients. Recently, I have followed my passion in helping organizations succeed by completing a data analytics and visualizations bootcamp program (UCI Division of Continuing Education).

My growing skill set has allowed me to transform data into useful, manageable tools that help discover trends, inform conclusions, and support decision-making processes. I treat datasets as informative puzzles that can be used to add value to any project or business. I enjoy both the challenge of understanding the complexities of a puzzle that others miss as well as realizing the definitive value I add with solving the puzzle for my team.

I can confidently say I would be a strong asset to any team and look forward to the challenges ahead.
